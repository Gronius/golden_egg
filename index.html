About the restaurant

A magical atmosphere, impeccable service, exquisite interior and, most importantly, a rich selection of delicious dishes of excellent quality - these are the characteristics that attract guests to our restaurant.

The waiters of this establishment know all the subtleties of cooking, they will tell guests the most suitable dishes for them. They know regular customers well, skillfully pleasing their tastes.

The restaurant is ideally complemented by a cozy coffee shop located on the first floor. A cup of amazing coffee and sweets from a local pastry chef will charge you with vivacity and positive energy.

Our team

CHEF

Our highly qualified chef is responsible for the selection of products and the preparation of delicious dishes. Dishes prepared by him turn into real masterpieces. No one will remain indifferent to such an unrivaled combination.

We want to please each of our visitors with our work. We guarantee all clients that the time spent in our establishment will be distinguished by special warmth and exquisite taste.

DUCK STORIES

Here, duck legs are marinated with herbs, baked, and then stewed until the meat acquires a tender texture.

The duck is served with red cabbage stewed in red wine, as well as mashed potatoes with cracklings. Farm duck breast is fried and served with demi-glace sauce and cherry coulis, as well as with tender potato gratin baked with cream, thyme and Parmesan cheese.
