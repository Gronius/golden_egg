About the restaurant

A magical atmosphere, impeccable service, exquisite interior and, most importantly, a rich selection of delicious dishes of excellent quality - these are the characteristics that attract guests to our restaurant.

The waiters of this establishment know all the subtleties of cooking, they will tell guests the most suitable dishes for them. They know regular customers well, skillfully pleasing their tastes.

The restaurant is ideally complemented by a cozy coffee shop located on the first floor. A cup of amazing coffee and sweets from a local pastry chef will charge you with vivacity and positive energy.
